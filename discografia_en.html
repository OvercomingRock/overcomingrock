[2017] CRISIS (Digital Version)

Crisis is the second album released by Overcoming, but the first one to be a long play (over 77 mins long). When it comes to the concept of the record, the lyrics are about decision making and human relationship problems. Produced by Juan Di Bernardo and Sebastián Luna. Mixed by Alejandro Planchart. Mastering by Martín Caballero at Pu Rec Studio, Formosa, Argentina. Artwork by Abstract Chaos Design .

[2017] CRISIS (Audio CD)

The physical CD version of Crisis, does not include the song "Mar de Ansenuza" because of a 73 min restriction given by Laser Disc, however it is included in the digital version and now available on the main streaming platforms. Produced by Juan Di Bernardo and Sebastián Luna. Mixed by Alejandro Planchart. Mastering by Martín Caballero at Pu Rec Studio, Formosa, Argentina. Artwork by Abstract Chaos Design . This album could be replicated thanks to a subsidy given by the Argentinian goverment through INAMU (Instituto Nacional de la Musica)
